Homemade Nachos

Recipe Prepraration

We can make these nachos from scratch using common uitilities of the kitchen.

Preparing the Nachos

  • First, cut the Masienda Tortillas into nacho chip shapes, typically cutting them into quarters.
  • Dry the cut tortillas in a low oven, at 200 degrees for 10 minutes. Tip: flip the chips halfway through for the most dryness.
  • Depending on how many chips you are making, you will need to fry them in a gallon of canola oil at 350 degrees fahrenheit for 1-2 minutes. Tip: The cut tortillas has very little natural flavor, making the oil used to fry them reusable.
  • After cooking, while the chips are removed from the oil and still darknening, season with a reasonable amont of kosher salt.
  • Let the fried chips dry over a paper towel on a pan until cool enough to eat.

Preparing the Guacamole

  • First, in a medium bowl, put the flesh of three small ripe avacados, a clove of crushed garlic, a small minced red onion, the juice of one lime, a teaspoon of ground cumin, a pinch of kosher salt and black pepper, 2 teaspoons of finely chopped cilantro, and half of a finely chopped jalepeno.
  • Mash together the contents of the bowl with a forkuntil the desired consistency is reached (I like my guacamole a little chunky).
